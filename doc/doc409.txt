Charities and Community Amateur Sports Clubs


          Contact HMRC if your Charity or Community Amateur Sports Club needs help on Gift Aid, Payroll Giving, tax reliefs, VAT or completing a Company or Self Assessment tax return

Child Benefit: general enquiries


          Contact HMRC for information about eligibility, claiming and stopping Child Benefit, changing your personal details and making a complaint

Child Trust Fund


          Contact HMRC online to find out where a Child Trust Fund is held, or if your child is terminally ill and you want to take money out of their fund or Junior Individual Savings Account

Childcare providers 


          Call or write to HMRC if you're a childcare provider and need help with Working Tax Credit
