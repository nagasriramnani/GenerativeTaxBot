Types of trust

The main types of trust are:

bare trusts

interest in possession trusts

discretionary trusts

accumulation trusts

mixed trusts

settlor-interested trusts

non-resident trusts

Each type of trust is taxed differently. Trusts involve a ‘trustee’, ‘settlor’ and ‘beneficiary’.
