Check how much Income Tax you paid last year

Once your Income Tax has been calculated, you can use this service to check how much you paid from 6 April 2022 to 5 April 2023.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) calculates everyone’s Income Tax between June and November.

You cannot check your Income Tax for last year if you paid any part of your Income Tax last year through Self Assessment.

Before you start

To check your Income Tax for last year, you’ll need to sign in to your personal tax account using your Government Gateway user ID and password.
