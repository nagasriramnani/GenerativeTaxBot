How much you can get

For the 2023 to 2024 tax year, Maintenance Payments Relief can reduce your tax bill by the lower of the following:

£401 - where you make maintenance payments of £4,010 or more a year

10% of the money you’ve actually paid - where you make payments of less than £4,010 a year

You cannot claim a tax reduction for any voluntary payments you make.
