Your payment may be delayed if you use the wrong reference number.

When you’re ready to pay:

Start your Class 2 National Insurance payment.

Select the ‘pay by bank account’ option.

You’ll then be directed to sign in to your online or mobile banking account to approve your Class 2 National Insurance payment.

The payment is usually instant but sometimes it takes up to 2 hours to show in your account.
