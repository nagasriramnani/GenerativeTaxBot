Make a complaint

Contact the relevant HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) helpline if you’re unhappy with the service you’ve received. HMRC has guidance about how they’ll deal with your complaint and what happens next.

There’s a separate process for:

complaints about HMRC online services

reporting serious misconduct by HMRC staff
