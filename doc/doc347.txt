Excepted estates - if the person died on or after 1 January 2022

You only need to report the value of the estate if you’re applying for probate. Not all estates need probate, so check if you’re not sure whether probate is needed.

Before you start, check that you’re dealing with an excepted estate and that you do not need to send full details of the estate.
