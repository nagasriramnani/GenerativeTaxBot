Possession Claim Online: recover property

You can take court action to repossess a property if you’re owed money for rent or a mortgage, and the tenant or mortgage holder will not pay.

You can only use this service if:

you’re over 18

you own a property in England

you’re owed money in rent or mortgage payments for a residential property

You cannot take court action to repossess a property if you’re told that someone is temporarily protected from creditors through the ‘Breathing Space’ scheme. Find out what to do when someone is using the ‘Breathing Space’ scheme.

You’ll have to pay £355 using a debit or credit card or Direct Debit.
