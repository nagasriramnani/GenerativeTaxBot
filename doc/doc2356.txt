Employers: general enquiries

Online

Tell HMRC if you have been payrolling benefits

Tell HMRC you do not owe Class 1A National Insurance contributions

Check if you can fix your problems with running payroll

If HMRC needs to contact you about anything confidential they’ll reply by phone or post.

Ask HMRC online

Ask HMRC’s digital assistant to get a quick answer to your query.

If the digital assistant cannot help you, you can ask to transfer to an HMRC adviser if they’re available.

Phone

Phone HMRC to get advice if you’re an employer.
