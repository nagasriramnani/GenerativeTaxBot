UK residents: EU tax debt


          Use the online forms, call or write to HMRC if you've been contacted about a tax debt from an EU member state

VAT: Annual Accounting and Flat Rate Schemes


          Write to HMRC to change your details if you're in the Annual Accounting or Flat Rate schemes, or want to leave the schemes

VAT: general enquiries


          Contact HMRC to report changes to your business or ask questions about VAT (including Making Tax Digital for VAT) and Fulfilment House Due Diligence Scheme (FHDDS)

VAT: insolvency


          Send your VAT Return to HMRC if you or your business becomes bankrupt or insolvent

VAT: option to tax


          Email, call or write to HMRC for help on deciding how to tax land or buildings for VAT purposes
