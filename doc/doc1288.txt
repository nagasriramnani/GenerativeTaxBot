Get help from the Insolvency Service

Get information on bankruptcy, redundancy, paying off your debts, company insolvency and misconduct of trading companies and partnerships.

With the Insolvency Service you can:

search the bankruptcy and insolvency register

read about options for paying off your debts

claim for redundancy in England, Scotland and Wales

make and serve a statutory demand, or challenge one

apply for bankruptcy

see what options are available if you cannot pay your debts

find an insolvency practitioner

You can get phone and email details for the Insolvency Service on their organisation page, if you need more information.

There is different guidance on insolvency and bankruptcy in Scotland and insolvency and bankruptcy in Northern Ireland.
