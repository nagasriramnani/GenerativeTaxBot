Before you start

If your property is in Wales

You will need to complete the forms and send them to your local county court.

You can pay the £355 fee using a debit or credit card or Direct Debit. You can also send a cheque or postal order, or pay at the court

When you send the forms, you will also need to include one of the following:

the help with fees reference number

a contact number if you want to pay the fee over the phone

If your property is in Scotland or Northern Ireland

If your property’s in Scotland you can use your local sheriff court.

There’s a different process in Northern Ireland.

Apply by post

You can also apply by post. You’ll need to download and fill in a claim form and return it to your local county court.
