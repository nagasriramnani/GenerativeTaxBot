Tell HMRC when you change your address

Use this service to update your address for:

Income Tax

National Insurance

State Pension

tax credits and Child Benefit

To sign in you need a Government Gateway user ID and password. If you do not have a user ID you can create one when you update your address.

You need to wait until you’ve moved before telling HMRC about your new address.

Before you start

You cannot use this service to update your details:

for business tax

if you only pay tax through Self Assessment

You’ll have to update your details a different way.

Personal tax account

Signing in also activates your personal tax account. You can use this to check your HMRC records and manage your other details.
