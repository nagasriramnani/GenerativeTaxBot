Defend the claim

You can defend the claim if either:

you do not think you owe the other person or business any money

you’ve already paid them

You can also make a claim against them (‘counterclaim’) if you think they owe you money. You might have to pay a court fee.

Download and fill in either:

form N9B if the claim is for a fixed (‘specified’) amount

form N9D if the claim is for an unspecified amount

You can respond online if the claim was made using an online service.

You’ll be offered mediation when you respond to the claim.
