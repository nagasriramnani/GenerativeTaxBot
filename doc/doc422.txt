National Insurance: non-UK residents


          Contact HMRC for advice on National Insurance and healthcare if you're under State Pension age and live or work abroad

National Yachtline


          Call the National Yachtline if you're sailing your yacht or pleasure craft to the UK

New Computerised Transit System


          Email or call HMRC for help with the New Computerised Transit System, Import Control System, Export Control System and using the online services

New Computerised Transit System: testing services


          Email HMRC if you're a software developer testing the New Computerised Transit System

Non-UK resident employees 


          Call or write to HMRC for tax advice if you're a non-UK resident and have been employed in the UK for up to 3 years
