Non-UK resident entertainers


          Call or write to HMRC for tax advice if you're a non-UK resident entertainer or sportsperson who performs or competes in the UK

Non-UK resident landlords


          Call or write to HMRC to register for the Non-resident Landlord (NRL) scheme or for help if you live outside the UK but have rental property there

Non-UK residents: Income Tax and Capital Gains 


          Contact HMRC for help with Income Tax or Capital Gains Tax if you live or work abroad

Non-UK residents: payment


          Contact HMRC if you live outside the UK permanently and need help paying your UK tax

Northern Ireland Corporate Tax Office 


          Call or write to HMRC for tax guidance about investing in or expanding your business in Northern Ireland
