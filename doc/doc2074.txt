VAT when you sell a vehicle

Work out what VAT to charge when you sell a vehicle that’s in Great Britain (England, Scotland and Wales). You must be a VAT-registered business to use this tool.

Adapted vehicles for wheelchair users are zero-rated (the rate of VAT you charge is 0%). If you’re selling one, check the rules and make a declaration.
