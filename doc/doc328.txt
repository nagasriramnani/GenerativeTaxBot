Before you apply

Before applying for probate you’ll need to complete the following steps.

Check that probate is needed and that you can apply.

Estimate the estate’s value for Inheritance Tax. Even if there’s no tax to pay, you’ll need the value as part of your probate application.

Start paying any Inheritance Tax that’s due and wait 20 working days before applying for probate.

If there’s no Inheritance Tax to pay and the person died on or before 31 December 2021

You must report the value of the estate to HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) online or by post.
