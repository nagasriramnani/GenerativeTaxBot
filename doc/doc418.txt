          Contact HMRC for help with questions about importing, exporting and customs reliefs

Income Tax: general enquiries


          Contact HMRC for help with questions about Income Tax, including PAYE coding notices and Marriage Allowance, and Class 4 National Insurance and for advice on your savings (including ISAs) and claiming tax back on interest

Inheritance Tax: general enquiries


          Call or write to HMRC for advice on Inheritance Tax following a death

Inheritance Tax: payments


          Post your Inheritance Tax payment to HMRC, or use the online form to get an Inheritance Tax reference number
